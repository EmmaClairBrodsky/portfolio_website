Covert Affairs

A 2D noir point-and-click RPG mystery game that immerses players in a shadowy world of intrigue and deception.

Play the Game

Play Covert Affairs on itch.io

Overview

Covert Affairs combines classic point-and-click mechanics with RPG elements to simulate reading a mystery novel. Players navigate through a noir-inspired world by physically interacting with words, turning dialogue and text into tangible objects that drive investigation and choice.

Key Features

  • Developed in Godot with a fully custom dialogue engine
  • Engineered a branching dialogue framework to manage narrative state, choices, and progression
  • Designed and produced all 2D artwork, sprites, and character designs
  • Integrated art, UI, and dialogue systems to support interactive storytelling

Technical Implementation

Covert Affairs was developed with a team of artists, storytellers, and programmers in CSC 486 - Computational Visual Narrative. All UI and design choices were made to support the overarching narrative and atmosphere of the game.
